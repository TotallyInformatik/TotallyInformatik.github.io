About "TT" - TotallyTuring

Hi reader, My name is Rui Zhang and I'm 14 years old.

In 2019, we learned HTML, CSS and JS - Webdevelopment, and honestly I had a blast! So I thought it would be a great idea to use the knowledge I have gathered on the Internet and at school to create a website with actual meaning. This is how "TT" - TotallyTuring was born.

I chose the name TotallyTuring, because Alan Turing was a renowned computer scientist and mathematician, among other things. It should symbolize how I also want to become a computer scientist one day. Some might find the name too arrogant, as it implies that I am tOtAlLy as smart as Alan Turing, but please keep in mind that it is still just sarcasm and no 14 year old can be as intelligent as Alan Turing was. (maybe a genius, but I don't believe that I am good enough to be called a genius) The two T's also look like the symbol for PI, and indicates the close relationship between math, computer science and programming.

On this site, I will try to post my recent discoveries concerning programming and computer science as often as possible. Since I am still in school, I won't have too much time for programming, because there are lots of other subjects that I have to study for too. Regardless, I will try my best to take most of my free time to improve my coding skills.
